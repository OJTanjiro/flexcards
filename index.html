Whatsapp

WhatsApp is an instant messaging application for smartphones. It allows users to send not only texts but also images, videos, audios, audio recordings (voice memos), documents, locations, contacts, gifs, and stickers.

Copilot

A copilot is a conversational, AI-powered assistant that helps boost productivity and streamline workflows by offering contextual assistance, automating routine tasks, and analyzing data.

Google Maps

Google Maps is a web mapping platform and consumer application offered by Google. It allows you to view satellite imagery, street maps, and panoramic views of places. It also helps you to find locations, calculate routes, and get directions for various modes of transportation.

Duolingo

Duolingo is a language-learning platform that offers free online courses to help individuals learn languages.

Zoom

Zoom is a cloud-based video communications app that allows you to set up virtual video and audio conferencing, webinars, live chats, screen-sharing, and other collaborative capabilities. It is a video conferencing platform that can be used through a computer desktop or mobile app.

Ring

Ring cameras are part of a broad suite of products designed for home security. They allow users to monitor their property through video feeds accessible via smartphones, tablets, or computers.
